"Ahhh, this porridge is just right," she said happily and she ate it all up. So, she tasted the last bowl of porridge. "This porridge is too cold," she said. So, she tasted the porridge from the second bowl. "This porridge is too hot!" she exclaimed. She tasted the porridge from the first bowl. Goldilocks was hungry. At the table in the kitchen, there were three bowls of porridge. She knocked and, when no one answered, she walked right in. Pretty soon, she came upon a house. She went for a walk in the forest. Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Goldilocks.
