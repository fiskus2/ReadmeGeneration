At the table in the kitchen, there were three bowls of porridge. She went for a walk in the forest. Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Goldilocks. Pretty soon, she came upon a house. "This porridge is too cold," she said. "This porridge is too hot!" she exclaimed. Goldilocks was hungry. So, she tasted the last bowl of porridge. "Ahhh, this porridge is just right," she said happily and she ate it all up. So, she tasted the porridge from the second bowl. She tasted the porridge from the first bowl. She knocked and, when no one answered, she walked right in.
