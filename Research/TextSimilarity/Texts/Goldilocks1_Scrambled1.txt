At the table in the kitchen, there were three bowls of porridge. So, she tasted the last bowl of porridge. Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Goldilocks. So, she tasted the porridge from the second bowl. She tasted the porridge from the first bowl. She went for a walk in the forest. "Ahhh, this porridge is just right," she said happily and she ate it all up. She knocked and, when no one answered, she walked right in. "This porridge is too hot!" she exclaimed. Goldilocks was hungry. "This porridge is too cold," she said. Pretty soon, she came upon a house.
